Hunt the Wumpus

Rules: You are in a cave of 20 rooms, connected by tunnels. The Wumpus is hiding in one of them. Each turn, you can move to a connected room. If you enter the Wumpus’s room, you get eaten. Try to avoid it!

You can smell the Wumpus if it’s in a neighboring room.

Type a room number you want to move to and click "Go".
